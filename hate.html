Documenting Hate submission form

There is no reliable data on hate crimes and incidents of racial bias, or the prevalence of either. In an attempt to collect data and stories on hate and racial bias incidents in our community, The Denver Post has joined ProPublica's Documenting Hate project.

When you fill out this form you are sharing your story with ProPublica, The Denver Post and a coalition of news organizations, civil rights groups and universities. Your information will not be shared outside the coalition without your permission, and it will not be reported to police. Thank you for your participation.
